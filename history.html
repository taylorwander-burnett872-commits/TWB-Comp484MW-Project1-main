Umbrella welcomes you to a tour of our history.

1960-1967

Oswell E. Spencer and Edward Ashford spearhead a small philanthropic pharmaceutical campaign in rural west Africa. Their goal is to deliver modern medicine to those most in need of it. While there, they begin researching and experimenting with local flora and fungi.

1968-1971

Working off of the substantial amount of experience and knowledge gained from their time in Africa, Spencer and Ashford establish Umbrella Pharmaceuticals in Berlin, Germany and formally establish an Umbrella branded research and development station in west Africa to continue their philanthropic work. Shortly following its establishment, co-founder Edward Ashford unfortunately passes away. The valiant Oswell E. Spencer vows to continue his friends work through Umbrella

1972-1977

Umbrella expands their pharmaceutical operations into the United States. Umbrella establishes their primary US branch in the midwestern metropolitan hub of Raccoon City.

1978-1980

Breakthroughs at the Arklay facility allow Umbrella to rapidly introduce a series of innovative and highly effective pharmaceuticals. Among these was the Umbrella patented First Aid Spray, which still sees heavy use all across the world today. Umbrella also begins to branch out into other industries with the establishment of Umbrella Solutions, a subsidiary focused on the development and production of state-of-the-art medical equipment.

1981-1985

Umbrella opens another facility located in Tokyo Japan, thus cementing them as the worlds #1 pharmaceutical supplier and producer. Branching even further out, Umbrella establishes Parasol, a general electronics focused branch. Umbrella makes the move to formally change their name from Umbrella Pharmaceuticals to simply Umbrella Corporation. The name Umbrella Pharmaceuticals remains as the name of their pharmaceuticals production and research branch.

1986-1990

Umbrella branches out into hospitality and entertainment, and with the Paraguas Line Company and the UTC Corporation respectively. Umbrella opens their second US facility in Chicago.

1991-Present

Umbrella's massive contributions to the infrastruce and economy of Raccoon City see it established as the formal 'home of Umbrella'. Umbrella Continues to provide for the community through innovation and pure good-will.
