Community Spotlight: Umbrella’s Partnership with Raccoon General Hospital


Umbrella stands as the number 1 worldwide pharmaceutical researcher and producer. While an impressive feat on its own, we here at Umbrella feel that doesn't mean much if these state of the art treatments and medicines aren't making their way into the hands of those that need them in a timely and efficient manner. That is why Umbrella is excited to showcase our recent partnership with Raccoon General Hospital. Through this partnerhsip, Umbrella hopes to deliver fast and effective brand new pharmaceuticals to those most in need of them.


Written by Anonymous| July 12, 1998

Umbrella Corporation proudly stands as the world’s leading pharmaceutical researcher and producer. Our laboratories operate at the cutting edge of science, developing innovative treatments designed to combat illness and improve quality of life across the globe. Yet even the most advanced breakthroughs hold little value if they cannot reach patients swiftly and efficiently.


That is why Umbrella is pleased to spotlight our growing partnership with Raccoon General Hospital—a collaboration grounded in shared values of medical excellence, rapid response, and community care.


A Lonstanding Relationship


Back in 1992, as a part of Mayor Warren's Bright Raccoon 21 initiative, it was formally announced that Raccoon City would be recieving a brand new, state-of-the-art general hospital. So as to show our support for Mayor Warren and the citizens of Raccoon City, Umbrella Corporation decided to become the primary finiancial backer of the project. Since then, we've remained relatively hands off in the operations and endevours of the hospital.


Recently however, The Umbrella Corporation has felt that we could do just a little bit more for the denizens of Raccoon City. Logically, us here at the Umbrella Corporation thought that the best place to begin would be at the Raccoon General Hospital, an establishment that we've already invested a fair bit into.


What This Means For Raccoon General


What this ultimately means for Raccoon General Hospital is that, going forward, Umbrella will provide the hospital with priority access to select newly developed pharmaceuticals, advanced treatment protocols, and research-backed therapeutic solutions.


This streamlined channel ensures that promising treatments move from laboratory to bedside with minimal delay. By reducing logistical barriers and enhancing communication between researchers and clinicians, we are helping accelerate patient access to innovative care.


Supporting Those on the Front Lines


Beyond pharmaceutical supply, Umbrella has also committed resources to ongoing training and knowledge exchange. Our medical liaisons collaborate with hospital staff to share insights into new formulations, recommended applications, and emerging best practices.


By fostering open dialogue between scientific researchers and frontline healthcare providers, we strengthen the bridge between discovery and delivery, thus ensuring that advancements are implemented safely, responsibly, and effectively.


Investing in the Future


Our final plan of action is to provide the hospital with a lump sum of $200,000. We hope that this money will prove invaluable in the continued operations of the hospital.

We Care About the Health of the Community


Umbrella believes that leadership in science carries with it a responsibility to serve. Partnerships like the one with Raccoon General Hospital reflect our broader commitment to the communities in which we operate. When local healthcare institutions are equipped with the latest tools and treatments, entire regions benefit.


As medical challenges evolve, so too must the systems that respond to them. Through collaboration, innovation, and shared purpose, Umbrella and Raccoon General Hospital are working side by side to provide faster, more effective care to those who need it most.


Together, we are building a healthier tomorrow—right here in Raccoon City. With our help, the help of The Umbrella Corporation, Raccoon City will shine as a beacon of health and safety throughout all of the World.
